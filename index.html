Who we are

We are a group of high schoolers passionate about game development. At Cupertino High School, GDC is a club that teaches how to create games. We operate mainly using Unity Engine and C#. We offer detailed lessons to teach other students how to create games. We cover a wide variety of topics, including Machine Learning.
