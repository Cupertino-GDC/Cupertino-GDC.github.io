Learn

We offer detailed lessons about programming in C#, the Unity game engine, example projects, and game development in general. We also cover a wide variety of other both STEM and non-STEM topics-- we welcome all different skill levels and interests! Whether you like art, writing, coding, sound design, or anything else, you'd belong right in GDC!


Lessons


Make

We also host Dev Challenges, where members are challenged to implement a small game mechanic to improve their coding and game development skills. We also host game jams for members to develop full games.

Share

In Game Analyses, members can play or watch a game selected by our officers and discuss what game design decisions the developers made. Members can also show off the games they made at game showcases hosted by the club.
