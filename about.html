Programs


Classes

Members will partake in a rigorous curriculum, allowing them to immerse themselves into the realms of Game Development and Programming. Through Unity and C#, members will learn through informative and easy-to-understand lessons on a weekly basis.




Teams

After learning the fundamentals Game Development and Programming, members will further solidify their understanding by participating in game-making projects in teams. Members will collaborate and compete in several game jams.
