Guest Speaker & McLeod Rap

5/11/21

For the final GDC meeting of the 2020/21 school year, we ended the year with a bang by hosting Jacob Mott, a guest speaker from Pysonix (developer of Rocket League) and Mr. McLeod, who dropped a few bars to finish off the year.


April 2021 Game Showcase

4/20/21

GDC's first and only game showcase of the 2020/21 school year! This was our first online showcase, where we displayed the games that various GDC Teams and officers have been working on.


ML Agents Workshop

3/30/21

The first ML Agents Workshop of many! This is the first time GDC has taught Machine Learning to members. By the end of the Unity ML Agents unit, members successfully created a rocket landing simulation, that accurately lands a rocket with an ideal landing force and velocity.


MelonJam

8/6/20 to 8/9/20

A fun, global, online, Game Jam for both beginners and advanced game developers. GDC created MelonJam with the intent of bridging the gap between high schoolers with a game development interest and experienced game developers. MelonJam collaborated with 7 sponsors, offered about $2000 in prizes, and massed about 200 participants!
