GDC's annual game showcase, where members presented their second semester game projects. This year's showcase featured 7 amazing projects across genres, from an origami arcade game to a wizard roguelike with sentient cutlery and a fighting simulator. We also had tons of food, including chips, mint oreos, and ramen! Members had fun playing the games, and we held a prize ceremony for the showcase winners.

ML Agents Workshop

The first ML Agents Workshop of many! This is the first time GDC has taught Machine Learning to members. By the end of the Unity ML Agents unit, members successfully created a rocket landing simulation, that accurately lands a rocket with an ideal landing force and velocity.
